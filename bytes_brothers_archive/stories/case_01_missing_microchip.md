# Case 01: The Missing Microchip

The Bytes Brothers are called in to investigate a missing prototype microchip from a laboratory.

## Suspects

1. Dr Smith
2. Ms Clark
3. Mr Jones

## Clues

- The thief entered after 18:00.
- Smith left at 17:00.
- Jones never entered the lab.

## Deduction

Smith is ruled out by time.
Jones is ruled out because he never entered the lab.
That leaves Clark as the only suspect with opportunity.

## Solution

Ms Clark stole the microchip.
